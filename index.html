Prompt Engineering

AI Tips That Actually Work

Skip the hype. These are battle-tested strategies we share on Instagram every week.

🎯

The T.C.R.E.I Framework

Structure every prompt with Task, Context, Role, Examples, and Intent. This framework consistently outperforms vague one-liners and gets professional-grade outputs.

❓

"Which AI is Smartest?" is the Wrong Question

In 2026, "best AI" is not a real answer. The right question is: which AI fits YOUR workflow? Each model has different strengths for different tasks.

🔬

Most Prompt Engineering Tricks are Placebos

Adding "think step by step" randomly, excessive role-playing prompts, and magic words rarely improve outputs. What matters is clarity, context, and constraint.

📚

Free Prompt Foundations Workbook

62 pages covering Chapters 1–6 of the T.C.R.E.I framework, prompt comparisons, and exercises. Follow @the.prompt.layer on Instagram to get your free copy.

⚙️

Use AI for Life Operations

Instead of asking AI random questions, build repeatable systems: weekly reviews, decision frameworks, research templates, and writing workflows that compound over time.

🔄

Chain Your Prompts

Single prompts are weak. Chain multiple prompts in sequence: research → outline → draft → edit → polish. Each step builds on the last for 10x better results.
